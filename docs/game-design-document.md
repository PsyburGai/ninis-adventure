# Nini's Adventure – Design Document

## Concept
A charming 2D pixel art platformer starring Nini, a small adventurer exploring vibrant worlds.

## Core Mechanics
- Run, jump, double-jump
- Collect items / coins
- Defeat enemies by jumping on them
- Reach the end-of-level flag

## Worlds (Planned)
1. **World 1** – Grasslands
2. **World 2** – Cave / Underground
3. **World 3** – Sky / Clouds

## Characters
- **Nini** – Player character
- **Enemies TBD**

## Controls
| Action | Key |
|--------|-----|
| Move Left | A / ← |
| Move Right | D / → |
| Jump | Space / ↑ |

## Art Style
- Low-res pixel art (16×16 tiles, 32px grid)
- Viewport: 320×180 (scaled to 1280×720)
- Warm, saturated palette per world

## Audio
- Chiptune-style SFX via ChipTone
- Looping background music per world
